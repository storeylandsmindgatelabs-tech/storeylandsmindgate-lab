How it works

Voice → AI → Spatial context → Full VR. The Synth engine interprets voice intent, creates spatial logic, and populates worlds in real time.

1. Speak a scene — describe vibe, scale, objects.
2. AI interprets intent and builds geometry, lighting, and audio.
3. AR/VR presentation with dynamic NPCs and collaboration tools.
